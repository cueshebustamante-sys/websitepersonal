General Info

The course includes web technologies, English communication, and HCI fundamentals. It balances theory with practical exercises.

Requirements

Students need a laptop, internet access, and a code editor (VS Code recommended). Devices should be fully charged before class.

Resources

Textbooks, lecture slides, and online tutorials will be provided. Extra reference materials can be found in the library.

Reminders

Assignments must be submitted on time, and students must attend at least 80% of classes. Late submissions may lower grades.
